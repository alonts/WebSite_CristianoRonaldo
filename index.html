Hey, this is me Ronaldo! In this section I will tell you about myself.

I Born and raised on the Portuguese island of Madeira.
The beginning of my career I began in Sporting CP , And then in 18 years old I signed in Manchester United.

In 22 years old ,I had received Ballon d'Or and FIFA World Player of the Year nominations and at age 23, I won my first Ballon d'Or and FIFA World Player of the Year awards.
In 2009, I was the subject of, what was, at the time, the most expensive association football transfer when I moved from Manchester United to Real Madrid in a transfer worth €94 million (£80 million).
With Real Madrid, I won 15 trophies, including two La Liga titles, two Copas del Rey, four UEFA Champions League titles, two UEFA Super Cups, and three FIFA Club World Cups. I become Real Madrid's all-time top goalscorer when I scored a record 34
La Liga hat-tricks, including a record-tying eight hat-tricks in the 2014–15 season[note 3] and is the only player to reach 30 goals in six consecutive La Liga seasons.
Ater I won in 2016 and 2017 Champions Leagues, I secured back-to-back Ballons d'Or again in 2016 and 2017.
A historic third consecutive Champions League followed, make me the first player to won the trophy five times.
In 2018, I signed for Juventus in a transfer worth an initial €100 million; the highest ever paid by an Italian club and the highest fee ever paid for a player over 30 years old!!!!
